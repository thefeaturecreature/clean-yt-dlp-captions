## TL;DR

AI is reshaping software engineering by introducing metrics like token usage, which some companies use for performance evaluations, leading to 'token maxing'—engineers artificially boosting usage to avoid low rankings. While AI boosts individual productivity, team-level gains are inconsistent due to poor integration. Roles are evolving, with engineers handling more responsibilities like DevOps and product tasks, while AI tools reduce team sizes. Large firms are investing heavily in custom AI infrastructure, prioritizing internal development over off-the-shelf tools for competitive advantage. Despite inefficiencies, early adoption is seen as critical for innovation and hiring top talent.
